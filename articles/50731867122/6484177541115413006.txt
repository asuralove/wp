<p><a class="image"  href="bytedance://large_image?url=http%3A%2F%2Fp9-tt.byteimg.com%2Flarge%2F436200012747e6bfdb75%3Ffrom%3Ddetail&index=1" width="640" height="360" thumb_width="120" thumb_height="68" zip_src_path="eJzMk9FqgzAUht_lXDsTMxdZYOxBxiimRg2YKjFWivjuxRYsNIZG24teivxfzvfnZIBay0IegA3QaQkMqlQXAsWflGCMI5LEiaA8z3jyBQF0umqB_UFpTMMQar4_jAn5yQipinBfK-RO_-a6Vj-ZMKmsIJgJ9GlCtJHwPwZgyk7xm_vl09fdOteddkxOLPnViK1DTPK94M3u_vqv_1t0jFBPY7xICtMpulCJ5eNP867ofZBzh95b5CyOWA_pEcQl9yrQitVcrGY8BwAA__-XsWIK"  ></a></p><p>在接受记者采访时，玛拉诺向大家分享了她的一个长寿小秘诀。这个秘诀就是“每天吃两个鸡蛋”，是不是听起来太简单了呢？但的确是这样。</p>

<p><a class="image"  href="bytedance://large_image?url=http%3A%2F%2Fp9-tt.byteimg.com%2Flarge%2F43600003eef36be6bdd4%3Ffrom%3Ddetail&index=2" width="640" height="426" thumb_width="120" thumb_height="80" zip_src_path="eJy8ktGKgzAQRf9lnl0TVwlsYOmHlFJMjRowVeJYKeK_F1uwUE2JVfooMidzz50OSqMydQbeQWMUcChik0kShYxSSkMp05AJyUSSROBBY4oa-B5yxIoTUv39IPriilLpzD-Vmtind6kp9X8iMVYFeCMhWE34ZR8iDr0HmDdaPMPfP13DT961T1tWn-j7GmHI3kpRHV_rf_yvySUgLYvoLMmPh9EZI5My3WnO5a5GbrXkqND5hqzeFrT43tZmoAW3PaumvwUAAP__6Phqjg=="  ></a></p><p>玛拉诺的医生告诉记者，她每天吃一点蔬菜，早上和中午各吃一个鸡蛋，晚上吃一点肉，这个习惯伴随了她100年。</p>

<p><a class="image"  href="bytedance://large_image?url=http%3A%2F%2Fp9-tt.byteimg.com%2Flarge%2F43660000f359466ff737%3Ffrom%3Ddetail&index=3" width="640" height="523" thumb_width="120" thumb_height="98" zip_src_path="eJzMkuHKgjAUhu_l_PZz89MmDqILCQmtTQcuZc4kxHsPCwzSgVt_-jnGeTjP-54BaiUKcQU6QKcEUKgyVTAUhYRgjDEPd0lECOdxGIMHnapaoEcotW4oQk3yp7Wf3zUTsvDPtUTm6QNXtdxfmM5EBd5M-CdfIwJHQjp6oMtO5m_559NZ3jy9dXNrgusOk3vP8ub0Wf_rv0W3APUkwqskP5tGVxJZlrkdZ7qPheHvIOcQN1-ROTmLIi3lHEGLKq046Tg-AgAA__9xhl0t"  ></a></p><p>鸡蛋含有丰富的营养物质，其中包括13种身体必需营养素、7克优质蛋白和5克脂肪。没有心血管疾病的人，一天吃1、2个是没有问题的，不会增加你身体里的胆固醇含量。</p>
